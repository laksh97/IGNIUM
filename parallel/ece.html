PAPER PRESENTATION

PAPER PRESENTATION

DETAILS

Eligibility:
The event is open to all regular B.E./ B.Tech/M.Tech Students.

TOPICS:
1.Speech and Image Processing
2.VLSI Design and Nanotechnology
3.Artificial Intelligence,Neural Networks and Fuzzy Logic
4.Embedded Systems and Robotics
5.RFID Applications
6.High Speed Electronic Devices
7.Advanced Communications
8.Others related to Electronics and Communications Engineering

Guidelines for Participants:
* The event will be conducted department wise.
* The event will be from on '23-03-2017'.
* The maximum number of members in a team is two for Technical Paper Presentation.
* Papers for technical paper presentation should be in IEEE standard, max 8 pages of A4 size, typed in "Times New Roman" font size 12.
* Send Soft copy of Abstract not exceeding 300 words in one page to mail ID: 'isteece17@gmail.com' on or before '15-03-2017'
* Selected papers will be intimated through mail/ web link latest by '21-03-2017'.
* Fill the registration form and send abstract and documentation to 'isteece17@gmail.com'
* Submit two hard copies of the full paper, bonafide/college ID photo copy at the time of registration on'23-03-2017'.
* Maximum time duration for the Paper Presentation is 8 minutes followed by 2 minutes of discussion.

Registration details:
• Only selected candidates for papers have to pay registration fee.
• Registration fee:Rs. '100' -per member Non ISTE member.
• Registration fee:Rs. '50' -per member ISTE member (carry ISTE proof).
• The registration fee includes lunch & snacks.
